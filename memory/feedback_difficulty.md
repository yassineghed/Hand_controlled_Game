For this game, prefer harder/faster/riskier designs over safer ones. The creator finds the current tuning boring to play themselves.

**Why:** They said directly: "i need to include in you thinking to make a fun challenging game (not easy because now its slow even i dont like playing it)". The existing code is full of softening knobs (speed scale 0.68, fairness bounce, long min-visible times, slow difficulty ramp) that make it uninteresting.

**How to apply:** When suggesting mechanics or tuning, default to the spicier option. Ramp difficulty aggressively. Reward skill, punish sloppiness. Don't propose padding, tutorials, generous hitboxes, or "accessibility" mitigations unless asked. Juice and feedback should make hard feel fair, not slow down the game.
